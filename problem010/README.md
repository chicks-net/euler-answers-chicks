Euler Problem 10
================

[Problem definition](http://projecteuler.net/problem=10):

> The sum of the primes below 10 is 2 + 3 + 5 + 7 = 17.
> 
> Find the sum of all the primes below two million.

## Notes

* apparently there are 148,933 primes below 2 million
